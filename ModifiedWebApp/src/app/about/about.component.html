Our Mission

Started in 2020, NISSE started as a project for CSCI 6030 Information Retrieval and Extraction with Professor Tabrizi at ECU. The idea behind NISSE was to provide users with a tool that they could use to learn more about the ingredients in the foods they interact with and provide the user of this tool with a way to query a database quickly to meet their objective.
